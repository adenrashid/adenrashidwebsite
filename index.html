About Me

Hey! I'm Aden.

I have always enjoyed tech. Ever since I was young, I loved computers. My brother and I would argue about who would get to spend more time on our family desktop whether it was to browse the internet, play games, solve puzzles, or learn more about what a computer could do.

I've also always loved playing creative sandbox games such as The Sims, RollerCoaster/Zoo Tycoon, and Minecraft. I liked the idea of creating and building, then being able to enjoy the end product.

As I got older I discovered Myspace. I would spend countless hours designing my webpage, customizing the HTML and CSS, and absolutely loving it. I have always been creative and I found that this was a great outlet for me.

When it came time to choose what I wanted to do, I went for dentistry because I thought it was a great blend of problem solving and creativity. I worked as a dentist for close to 3 years, but found that I wanted more from my career.

At the same time that I was working as a dentist, I was teaching myself to code. I was doing tutorials on Codecademy, watching videos on Youtube, building my personal website, working on small problem solving projects, and loving it.

The problem solving aspect of dentistry as well as the attention to detail tied in perfectly with coding, and for me it was a natural progression.

I didn't realize that I could make what I've always enjoyed into my career, until now. I've never felt more passionate about who I am or what I want to do.

I believe I am fun, hard-working, and determined. I'm easy to work with, a really quick learner, and I get things done!
